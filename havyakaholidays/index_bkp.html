Explore the unexplored - Village Stay at Murudeshwar

25th - 26th Aug 2018

Murdeshwar is a town in Bhatkal Taluk of Uttara Kannada district in the state of Karnataka, India. Murdeshwar is another name of the Hindu god Shiva. Famous for the world's second tallest Shiva statue.

Uniqueness of Havyaka Holidays:

  • Stay at one of the ancient Havyaka house in near by village. (~15Kms from Murudeshwar)
  • Village is located at the foot hills of Western ghats surronded by forest and betel nut trees
  • Taste Havyaka food(Pure Veg).
  • Explore and trek around the village.
  • Explore the natural streams and waterfalls
  • Experience hospitolity of Havyaka family

Tour & Stay Attractions:

  • Visit Murudeshwara Temple & Beach.
  • Stay at ancient house(~300yrs old), constructed on over 8000 sqft area.
  • Natural spring water flowing 24/7.
  • Ancient Wooden Jhoola (6ft X 4ft).
  • Water stream near the house, one could take bath.
  • Early morning trek(easy) and witness beautifull sunrise.
  • You could spot peackocks & other beatifull birds.
  • Cow shed with ~10 Indian breed(Desi) cows.
